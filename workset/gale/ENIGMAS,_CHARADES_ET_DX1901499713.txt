Is there any one who can deny it? The mind of the strong man may be fully burdened. To him at last comes greater sorrow. In consequence he is found upon a bed of straw in a madhouse, which is a lesson to us all. The heart breaks as well as the back." The philosopher was right. Camels have so died. "Let us go back," said my friend. A word taken from each of the above sentences forms a well-known proverb, while the sentences themselves render the sense of the proverb. CYRIL BELLINGHAM.
