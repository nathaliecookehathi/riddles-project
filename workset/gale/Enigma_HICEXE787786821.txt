Enigma.
I am composed of IS letters. My 1, 4, 5, 8,12, IS, is useful in writing. My 2, 3, 10,11, is a band instrument. My 9, S, 11, 14, is a girl's name. My 13, 4, 7, is to tease. My 6, S, is all that is not out. My whole is a future event of special interest to residents of Arizona. Answer to last week's enigma: "Our Next President."
