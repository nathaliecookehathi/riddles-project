CHARADE.
My first for necessity is oftentimes placed ; When used as a herb, it means working of paste. A son of old Noah gives names to my second, (A joint with chickens a favourite reckoned). I'm sure after that, I need not say more, By my last you have guessed me a bit of a bore; I envy the compound, severe though I prove, Who basks in your smiles, and is blessed with your love.
