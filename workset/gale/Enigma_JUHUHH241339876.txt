Enigma
I am composed of nineteen letters. My 5, 18, 7, 11, is worn on the finger. My 8, 9, 10,11, is the title of a ruler. My 19, 6, IS, 3, is less than ten. My 12, 17, 1, is the opposite of thin. My 4, 5, 13, 14, 11, 2, 16, is what farmers and cattlemen dread. My whole is an ornament on the lawn of the Girls' Home. Last Week's Enigma—My whole is what the printers show when they work through a half holiday. Answer—Real manliness.
