CHARADES.
My first is what a swindler does when he's inclined to cheat, My second is a pretty flower, which smells not very sweet, My third's a far-famed bird of prey, that flies both high and fleet, And my answer in these lines you'll find, I did three times repeat. My first is a liquid both wholesome and pure, To be confined in my second I could not endure, That my tout it is useful you'll say I am sure.
