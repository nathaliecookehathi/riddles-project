All'regular readers of the Bible-and you are surely one-have their favourite texts, verses, and chapters, and it is very easy for most of them to find their favourite psalm. Our com- petition this month will be very simple for com- petitors, but very difficult for those who have to act as judges. Copy out your favourite psalm from the English Authorised Version of the Bible. Prizes will be awarded for those copies of favourite psalms (made with pens and black ink on white paper with lines) which are the best written, most correctly spelt, and most carefully finished. Write on one side of the paper only, and add your full name, age, address, and date of posting at the end. Each competitor is entitled to send one written copy of her or his favourite psalm. All competitions must reach the Editor before or by October 10th, and be entirely and in every respect honest, unaided work. The prizes-well- bound copies of the Psalter-will be awarded in two divisions--() to competitors under thirteen years of age, (2) to competitors who have reached their thirteenth birthday and are still under eighteen years of age at the time of sending in their competitions.
