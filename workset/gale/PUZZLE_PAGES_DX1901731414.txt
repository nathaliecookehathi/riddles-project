26.-PUZZLE. All hail to thee, O Liberty ! Despots must quail before thee now; Each tyrant quakes and fain would fly, And abject at thy shrine would bow. . . Dread goddess, ever bright and fair! Slaves are made free at thine approach; Each bondsman leaps into the light- Leaps joyous; utters no reproach, Lest Liberty should suffer in the nation's sight. Thus far: now please to solve my riddle, 'Tis neither cock and bull nor cat and fiddle, But just what people very often see When they toss up their caps for Liberty. 27.-DOUBLE ACROSTIC.
