" SEARCH TEXT" RULES. READ CAREFULLY, AND--KEEP. 1. You may join in the"'Search Text' Com- -petition for this year, provided you were UNDER 15 years of age on New Year's Day. 2. Your JANUARY and FEBRUARY Texts must reach the Editor not later than the last day of MA/RCH; Your -MARCH Texts must reach the Editor by the last day of APRIL ; Your APRIL Texts must reach the Editor by the last day of MAY; and so on. This rule does not: apply to Searchers who live abroad. 3. You must find the texts yourself, without .anyone's assistance. You must not use a Concordance or any other Bible Help, except an ordinary Reference Bible in the Authorised Version. 4. When you find a text occurring in more than one passage, give one only. 5. Use ink-not pencil-in filling up the lists, and use Arabic figures (1, 2, 3, 4), and not Roman numerals (I.,-II., III., IV.). 6. Write your full name, age, and postal address at the. end of each month's list, adding also the exact. date when the list is posted. 7. Tear off the complete page of the cover on which the texts are printed (do not send in the whole copy of the Gleanings); fold it up, and put it in an envelope, then turn -in the flap. Don't seal or stick or fasten the envelope, and then you need only put on a halfpenny stamp- unless you enclose a letter, which will need a .penny stamp. 8. Address all lists and letters to the Literary Superintendent, Bible House, 146, Queen Victoria Street, London, E.C. 9. Remember, we only print the names of those who do not make even the smallest mistake. Answers received by the 15th of any month will.., be acknowledged in the Gleanings published for the second month after. Thus-- Lists.received by 15th of January will be acknowledged in March; Lists received by 15th of February will Le acknowledged in April; Lists received by 15th of March will be acknowledged in May; and so on. If your name does, not appear in due course, it means that you had some answer wrong or left out.
