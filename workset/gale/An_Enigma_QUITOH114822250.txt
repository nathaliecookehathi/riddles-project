An Enigma.
My first is in cat but not in kitten; My second is in bait, but not in bitten; ■' ■ My third is in mat, but not in rug; My fourth is in vase, but not in mug; My fifth is in learn, but not in teach; My sixth is in burn, but not in preach; My seventh is in arrow, but not in bow; My eight is in catch, but not in throw; My last is in walk, but not in ride. Now look at your valley's eastern side, To find where Nature has me abide. M.R. Answer to last week's enigma; Santa Fe Time,
