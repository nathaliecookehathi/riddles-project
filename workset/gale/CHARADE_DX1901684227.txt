CHARADE.
My first, then, I hope you will always remain, Removed from all trouble and torturing pain; In the depth of the ocean my second is seen, And it forms a dish fit to serve to a queen; My third is respected by bucks of the town, And I think now my tout should be very well known ; 'Twill declare, my dear friend, to your readers a name That's nobly recorded in annals of fame.
