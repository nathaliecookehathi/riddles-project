One of a chosen apostolic band. Where fell a thousand, slain by one strong hand ? Who by valour won his ladylove ? The prize for which some rival herdmen strove ? A favoured tribe, possessing west and south. A lukewarm Church, spurned from the Master's mouth. A sweet oasis, where each precious tree And bubbling well were noted carefully.
