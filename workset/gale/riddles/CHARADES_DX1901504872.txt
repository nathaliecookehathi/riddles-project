CHARADES. I.
Y first is what all boys should do If tasks to them are given; My second is a piece of string, But easy to be riven. My whole of unity doth tell. And is a word you know quite well. IT., My first in a shop you will constantly see; My second in windows will certainly be ; And my whole on a bed without doubt you will see. III. Chains are my first, my next a human being, My whole a slave who probably wants freeing. IV. My first 's an animal with horns, My second is a flock, My whole the onevho tends them all Through pasture and by rock. J. G.
