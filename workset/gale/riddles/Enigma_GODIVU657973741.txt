Enigma.
I am composed of eight letters. My 1, 6, 7, 2 was a child of our first parents. My 4, 5, 8 is worn on the head. My 1, 3, 7 is a South American woodsman, or means to copy. My 4, 1, 8, 7 is a bad feeling to have in one's heart. My whole, though of eight letters, when it belongs to the English language contains twenty-six. Answer to last week's enigma: Oratorical Contest. Answer handed in by Joseph Ball and Ada Baldwin.
