RI D DLE-E-REES.
MY first is a name, my second a child, My whole is unruly, ill-mannered, and wild. MY second stuck in my first and died; My whole is found in the country side. MY second went in my first to buy my whole, And then laid it on the floor, and stamped upon it. WHEN is a fisherman a good-natured fellow ?-When he gives place (plaice) to all the rest. WHEN does papa take his shortest walk ?-When he walks about in his old boots, or runs in his slippers.
