BIBLE ENIGIVA.
My first is a spice odoriferous and sweet; My second a cured one whom Peter did greet; My third is a title Christ Jesus doth bear; A call to true "thirsters" my fourth will appear; My fifth is a river to ancients well known; My sixth is the name of a valuable stone. By initials the name of a saint will appear The finals its signification makes clear.-G. W,
