CHARADE BY PORSON, TIHE CELEBRIATD GEGREEK SCHOLAR.
My first, though the best and most faithful of friends, You ungenerously name with the wretch you despise; My second-I speak it with grief-comprehends All the good, and the great, and the just, and the wise; Of my whole I have little or nothing to say, Except that it marks the departure of day. Answer :-Curfew.
