RIDDLE-ME-REES AND THEIR ANSWERS.
SHAT letters drain part of the South of England, South Wales, and North Wales ?-X, Y, D (the Exe, the Wye, and the Dee). What part of a fish is like the end of a story ?-The fin is (finis). When you stole my first I lost my second; but I hope you will always possess my whole. -Heartsease. What foot is that which has no toes ?- The foot of a hill. What band is that which has no buckle ?- A band of soldiers.
