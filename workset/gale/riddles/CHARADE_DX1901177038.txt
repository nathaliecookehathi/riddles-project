MY first expresses well the state Of those who rage,-and rave, and hate. My second heads the twenty-six ' Whb help us wisdom's lore to fix. My third in house, and hall, and way, Tqrns darkest night almost to day. My fourth flies swiftly o'er the land, And bears you to the utmost strand.
