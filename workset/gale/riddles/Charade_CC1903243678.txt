Near the fair ones my place is--no place could be better-- They owe me so much ! In each fraud their abettor, What would fall I support, I make firm what would yield; The sharpest of angles, the bone most revealed, By my aid to a delicate outline gives place, And each very defect wears an elegant grace. In the cunningest folds I the secrets conceal Which indiscreet eyes fain would have me reveal. Illusion is better, believe me :-then try, Not to know what I hide, but to guess who am I ? A. W. C.
