February Prize Competition; Result, We have received a very large number of lists of proper names in the Authorised Version of the Bible, which can be made out of the Society's telegraphic address (" Testaments, London"); most of them are very neatly written, and, with few exceptions, the rules of this competition have been carefully followed by those who took part. The longest list contained eighty-six words, but only fifty-two of them could be allowed as proper names. The shortest list was made up of sixteen names, all correct. In some lists, words and- names were given in which a letter was used several times, although it occurred only once in the address; in other lists names were duplicated.
