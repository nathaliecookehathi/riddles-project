Result of November Competition for School Boys and Girls (Under Seventeez Years of Age). This is the task our competitors accepted: to take the letters in the three words, Bible Society Gleanings, and form out of these letters alone the greatest possible number of geogra- phical names of countries, places, rivers, moun- tains, &c. &c., that occur in the Authorised Version of the Scriptures. We have received some very creditable lists, and many of them are well arranged and neatly written The rules have been carefully observed with just a few exceptions. Not one of the lists contains the correct and complete number of names-all have some omissions or include words which are not allowable.
