Latin Version of " Abide with me," I HAVE been greatly disappointed to receive so very few attempts at a literal English rendering of these rhymed Latin verses. A prize Bible is awarded to ENID JONES (age 14 ), 34, Bedford Gardens, Campden Hill, London, W. No boy, however, has sent in a translatioh of sufficient merit for a prize. I am afraid Christ- mas Examinations and Holidays have swallowed up my readers' leisure, or else Latin must be going out of fashion. Yet one school-girl (G. D.) writes to beg for a competition in Greek, which " she likes very much." I am delighted to hear of her good taste. But I am afraid she would not find many competitors. However, I will print in March a favourite German hymn, to see if my readers have more skill at modern than at ancient languages.
