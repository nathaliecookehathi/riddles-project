CHARADES.
In my first when young I took delight, And spent the summer day till night, To face my second I ne'er was dismayed, Though it keeps me afar from my own pretty maid; My tout is a warrior found at Waterloo, So find it out for me you witty few. Put a thousand to pay, and a thousand to key, As close as you can to their centers, And as sure as daylight you will have then sight, The greatest of all the tormenters.
